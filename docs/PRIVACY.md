# Privacy Operations

PrepCzar processes account, billing, subscription, study activity, and support data to operate the product.

## Data Minimization

Collect only data needed for:

- authentication
- billing
- subscription access
- practice history
- analytics
- support
- administrator content workflows

## Sensitive Data

Do not upload sensitive health information, client records, or confidential documents into content import workflows unless a lawful basis, user authorization, and operational need exist.

## AI Processing

Normal MCQ and flashcard practice does not call AI. Admin AI workflows may use content metadata, drafts, and import snippets for generation/review. Do not send secrets, payment details, or confidential personal data to AI providers.

## Payments

Stripe processes card data. PrepCzar stores Stripe customer/subscription identifiers and subscription status, not full card numbers.

## User Requests

Users may contact support for account help, export, correction, or deletion requests. Verify account ownership before fulfilling requests.
